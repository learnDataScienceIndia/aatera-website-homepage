Got Questions? We Have Answers

Find answers to common questions about our recycling services, processes, and compliance standards.

We recycle all types of batteries including lithium-ion, lead-acid, nickel-cadmium, nickel-metal hydride, and alkaline batteries from industrial, automotive, and consumer sources. Our processes ensure safe dismantling and maximum material recovery in compliance with UAE environmental regulations.
Our e-waste recycling process includes secure collection, transportation, dismantling, material segregation, and responsible recycling. We handle all types of electronic waste including computers, servers, networking equipment, printers, and telecom devices with full data security protocols.
ITAD is a comprehensive approach to managing the end-of-life cycle for IT equipment. Our services include asset auditing, secure data destruction, equipment refurbishment and remarketing, and responsible recycling — all documented with certificates for your compliance records.
We employ multiple data destruction methods including physical hard drive shredding, SSD destruction, degaussing, and certified software-based data sanitization. Every process is documented and clients receive a Certificate of Data Destruction for their records.
Yes, we offer flexible scheduled collection services tailored to your business needs. Whether you require daily, weekly, or monthly pickups, we can set up a recurring collection schedule that works for your operations across any location in the UAE.
Our services directly support your ESG goals by providing documented waste diversion from landfill, carbon emission reduction reports, certified recycling processes, and complete audit trails. We help businesses meet UAE sustainability targets and international ESG reporting standards.
Yes, we provide comprehensive Certificates of Recycling for all processed materials. These certificates include detailed breakdowns of materials collected, processing methods used, and recycling outcomes — essential documentation for your environmental compliance records.
